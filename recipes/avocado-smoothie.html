Avocado Smoothie

Description

What Is an Avocado Smoothie?

An avocado smoothie is a creamy and nutritious drink that combines the richness of avocado with the sweetness of fruits and a touch of honey. It's perfect for a quick breakfast or a refreshing snack.

You can add protein powder or Greek yogurt to make it more filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Scoop avocado flesh into a blender; add milk, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes. Blend ingredients until smooth.
